who we are ?

I'm Norah Johnson. I am the owner of Norah's Ceramics. I have been dealing with ceramics since childhood. My dad was a great artist. He was mainly involved in ceramics but also created in glass. In 2018, I founded my company and since then I have been trying to create beautiful things without impacting the natural environment. I invite you to my world and I hope you will love it as much as I do.

taking packaging seriously

We want to help end unnecessary packaging waste when shopping online. We're committed to using minimal, environmentally friendly postage packaging and keeping the amount of plastic used for products in general to an absolute minimum.

help us to help

We donate 5% of our income to charity every year. In addition, we organize free classes for people with disabilities. We love to awaken the spirit of art in people's souls!
